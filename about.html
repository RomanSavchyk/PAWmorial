Our Story

Hi there, my name is Michelle Lam. I am a veterinarian but I am also a pet mom The story of Pawmorial starts with being both a veterinarian and a pet owner. Having been on both sides of the euthanasia process, I know how difficult it is to lose your pet.

A few years ago, I lost my beloved rescue greyhound, Magical Ghost, to a very painful illness. He was y first dog and as all of our pets do, he had a huge impact on my life. It was a very difficult loss for me and my husband.

I create Pawmorial as a way for owners to capture all the happy moments they have shared with their pets. For some, this process might start after the loss of their pet, for others it might start when their pet is diagnosed with a terminal illness. I don’t think its ever too early to start celebrating your pet’s life, so I welcome everyone to create a memorial profile for their pets. I hope the users of the website will feel as much fulfillment as I did when I created the memorial profile for my pets.

Pawmorial was created for pet owners as a space to collect memories, new and old. Photographs and videos have the transformative power to bring us back to cherished moments in time. Our website that is geared towards helped pet owners strengthen the human-animal bond between them and their beloved pets.

This website prioritized the experience of owners, so if you have any suggestions that would make your experience better, please contact us at info@pawmorial.com
